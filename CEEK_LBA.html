Priority Areas


The Life Balance Assessment is tailored to that which you deem to be important. The list below suggests potential priority areas in your life. We encourage you to add or remove from this list to specify those areas of your life that you believe are most significant to you. You must select no fewer than four and no more than ten priority areas. Based upon the priority areas that you select, you will be asked to respond to a series of questions that will help identify sources of tension that might exist in relation to your priorities.
